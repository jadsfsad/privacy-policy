Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the FB Autoreply application ("the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts that have integrated the Service.

1. Information We Collect

When you send a message or post a comment on a Facebook Page or Instagram account that uses the Service, we receive the following information through Meta's webhook system:

  • Your Facebook or Instagram user ID (Page-Scoped ID)
  • The content of your message or comment
  • The Page or account you are interacting with
  • Timestamp of the interaction

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal contact information unless you voluntarily provide them in a message.

2. How We Use Information

The information we receive is used solely to:

  • Generate and send automated replies to your messages and comments
  • Operate basic logging for troubleshooting and service quality
  • Forward sensitive or out-of-scope inquiries to the Page owner for human review

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party providers:

  • Anthropic Claude API — generates the text of automated replies. Message content is sent to Anthropic for processing. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Platforms (Facebook & Instagram) — delivers messages between users and the Service. See Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Render — hosts the Service infrastructure. See Render's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Message content is processed in real time and is not stored in any persistent database by the Service. Temporary logs (which may include message excerpts) are retained for a maximum of 30 days for operational and debugging purposes, after which they are automatically deleted.

5. Data Security

The Service uses industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all communication, HMAC-SHA256 signature verification for incoming webhooks, and secure storage of API credentials.

6. Your Rights

You may request the following at any time by contacting us (see Section 8):

  • Confirmation of whether we have processed any of your data
  • Deletion of any logs containing your interactions
  • To opt out of further automated replies (you can also block or stop messaging the Page directly)

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
